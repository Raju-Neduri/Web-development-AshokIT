About St. Thomas School

St. Thomas E/M High School Jammikunta has been providing quality education since its establishment in 2001. Our school is committed to fostering academic excellence, character development, and overall growth of our students.

Our mission is to create a nurturing learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and a sense of community. With dedicated educators and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to prepare our students for a successful future.

Core Values

At St. Thomas School, our core values guide us in providing a well-rounded education. We emphasize:

  • Academic Excellence
  • Character Development
  • Community Engagement

  • Library with a vast collection
